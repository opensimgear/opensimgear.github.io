## Common actuator categories

- small motors for control feel
- direct-drive motors
- linear actuators
- rotary motion systems
- pressure and belt-driven cueing systems

## What matters

Speed, control quality, backlash, compliance, travel, noise, payload, and safety all shape the result. A stronger actuator is not automatically a better one.

## Why it matters

These ideas appear in wheel bases, motion systems, belt tensioners, and G-seat designs.
