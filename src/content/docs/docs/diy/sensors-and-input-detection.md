## Common sensor types

- potentiometers
- Hall-effect sensors
- load cells
- pressure sensors
- encoders and switches

## What matters

The important differences are repeatability, durability, noise sensitivity, calibration drift, mechanical integration, and whether the sensor is measuring position, force, or pressure.

## Why it matters

Sensor choice shapes the feel and maintenance profile of pedals, joysticks, throttles, shifters, and button interfaces.
