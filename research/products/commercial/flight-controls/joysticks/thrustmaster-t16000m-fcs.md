key_specs:

- Four independent axes including twist rudder.
- H.E.A.R.T Hall Effect magnetic sensors on pitch and roll.
- 16 action buttons, 8-way POV hat, ergonomic trigger.
- Ambidextrous grip can be set up for right or left hand use.

compatibility:

- PC.
- Thrustmaster page lists DCS, X-Plane 11/12, Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020, Star Citizen, Elite Dangerous, and War
  Thunder.

software/driver ecosystem:

- Compatible with Thrustmaster T.A.R.G.E.T advanced programming software.
- Can be combined with TWCS throttle, TFRP rudder pedals, and MFD Cougar panels.

strengths:

- Contactless sensors at budget-mid price.
- Left/right hand conversion supports dual-stick space setups.
- Better precision ceiling than most entry sticks.

limitations:

- Mostly plastic desktop unit.
- Twist yaw and small base are less ideal for cockpit mounting.
- Button layout is not aircraft-specific.

fit_notes:

- Strong mainstream "precision desktop stick" reference for joystick docs.
- Useful bridge between low-cost consumer sticks and modular enthusiast systems.
