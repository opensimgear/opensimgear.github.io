- Best fit for first tactile rig or single-seat cockpit.

limitations:

- Single channel unless multiple kits or amps are added.
- Less headroom than Gamer PRO, Advance, or LFE on heavy frames.
- Telemetry use depends on HaptiConnect or third-party software support.
