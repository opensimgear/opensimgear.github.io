- key_features:
  - Turns Arduino Micro plus HX711 into inexpensive USB pedal control board.
  - Load-cell brake, potentiometer gas and clutch, plus 12 additional buttons.
- hardware_or_bom:
  - Arduino Micro, HX711, load cell for brake, potentiometers for gas/clutch, optional buttons, existing or DIY pedal
    mechanics.
- firmware_or_software_stack:
  - Arduino sketch for USB joystick output.
- build_requirements:
  - Wire load cell through HX711, potentiometers to analog inputs, buttons to digital inputs, and flash Arduino Micro.
  - Calibrate ranges in firmware or OS/game [uncertain].
- compatibility:
  - PC USB joystick/game controller.
  - Works with sims that accept standalone pedal axes.
- strengths:
  - MIT license.
  - Simple way to retrofit load-cell brake into DIY or salvaged pedals.
  - Extra buttons can handle wheel/panel inputs.
- limitations:
  - Minimal documentation.
  - No pedal mechanical design.
  - Filtering/calibration depth unclear.
