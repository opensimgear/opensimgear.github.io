- key_features:
  - 6-speed H-pattern plus reverse.
  - Switches to sequential in about 3 seconds.
  - Optional clutch lock/eClutch mode.
  - Adjustable front-rear and left-right feel.
  - Multiple mounting points, side/underside mounting, handbrake adapter, and pedal connection possibility.
  - Mods include eClutch, button plate, gear indicator, button box, joystick, handbrake, and RHD variants.
- hardware_or_bom:
  - 3D printed gearbox body, mechanical shifter parts, Arduino Micro, switches/sensors, hardware, optional
    handbrake/eClutch/indicator/button mods.
  - Official shopping list and 3D files linked from docs; printed kits sold separately.
- firmware_or_software_stack:
  - Arduino code from Lebois documentation.
  - Optional SimHub integration for eClutch and other mods.
- build_requirements:
  - Extensive 3D printing with per-file print settings, assembly, wiring, Arduino flashing, and mechanical tuning.
  - Recommended to build base gearbox before mods.
- compatibility:
  - PC USB shifter through Arduino.
  - Console support requires third-party adapters and is uncertain.
- strengths:
  - One of the most complete DIY shifter ecosystems.
  - H-pattern and sequential in one device.
  - Strong community and mod catalog.
- limitations:
  - License unclear and some kits/files may be tied to store/Patreon/community distribution [uncertain].
  - Complex print/assembly compared with simple sequential shifters.
  - Documentation spread across many pages and versions.
