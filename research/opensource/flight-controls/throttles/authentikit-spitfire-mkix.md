key_features:

- Replica Spitfire MkIX control ecosystem: spade grip flight stick, throttle quadrant, trim/rudder-bias wheels, flaps
  lever, gear lever, gunsight, and rudder pedals.
- Controls are compatible with all flight simulators according to AuthentiKit.
- Community commonly flies MSFS, IL-2, and DCS.
- Universal Hub provides USB input support across controls.
- Stick and throttle use Hall sensors; stick includes brake lever, fire buttons, sealed bearings, hydraulic roll
  damping, and strong pitch return force.
