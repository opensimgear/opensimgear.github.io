- key_specs:
  - Toolless mode switch between sequential shifter and handbrake.
  - 20 degree handbrake movement and dual-stage spring mechanism.
  - Height-adjustable handle, table clamp included.
  - 157 mm body height, 359 mm max handle height, 114 x 136 mm footprint, 1625 g device weight, 2.5 m cable.
  - USB wired; aluminum grip.
- compatibility:
  - PRO Series and G Series wheels.
  - Windows 10/11; PlayStation/Xbox when connected to compatible Logitech wheel.
  - Cannot connect two units simultaneously in some contexts and handbrake response may be digital in limited cases per
    Logitech page.
- software/driver ecosystem:
  - Logitech G HUB for sequential actuation point and handbrake response curve.
- strengths:
  - Affordable 2-in-1 device.
  - Includes clamp and works with Logitech console ecosystem.
  - Useful for users alternating rally sequential and handbrake duties.
- limitations:
  - One unit cannot be both dedicated sequential and handbrake at same time.
  - Less premium than load-cell handbrakes.
  - Compatibility caveats with older G wheels/adapters.
- fit_notes:
  - Buy two only after confirming simultaneous-device support for your wheel/game path.
