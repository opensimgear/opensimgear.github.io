key_specs:

- 4 ohm large low-frequency transducer.
- 5-200 Hz response.
- 400 W minimum / 1500 W maximum power handling.
- 3.75 lb piston; 11 lb unit.

compatibility:

- Requires high-power external amplifier.
- Works with audio LFE, SimHub, HaptiConnect audio paths, and multi-channel shaker systems.
- Better for platforms, heavy cockpits, large seat bases, and simulator floors.

software/driver ecosystem:

- Passive device; no direct driver.
- Needs careful filtering and amp gain when driven from telemetry software.

strengths:

- Very high output for large structures.
- Deep low-frequency capability.
- Can replace several weaker shakers when used as a global low-frequency channel.

limitations:

- Overkill for light seats and apartments.
- High amp requirement raises system cost.
- Global mounting can smear separate left/right/front/rear cues.

fit_notes:

- Best as dedicated low-frequency seat/platform channel, not as tiny-zone shaker.
- Use mechanical isolation and an emergency gain limit in software.
